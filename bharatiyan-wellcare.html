Who We Are

Forged in Service.
Built for Care.

Bharatiyan Wellcare Systems is a veteran-owned medical equipment enterprise dedicated to improving patient outcomes through reliable respiratory care devices, surgical equipment, and accessories — delivered with the discipline and integrity of India's armed forces.

Founded by two distinguished veterans, we supply, service, and rent industry-leading brands including Resmed, Philips, BMC, Oxymed, Longfian, Drive, Inogen, and more — serving patients and healthcare providers across Bengaluru with the best in class.

What We Offer

Our Services

01
CPAP & BPAP Devices

Top-of-the-line continuous and bi-level positive airway pressure devices for sleep apnea and respiratory therapy. Brands: Resmed, Philips, BMC, Oxymed, Longfian.

02
Oxygen Concentrators

Stationary 5L & 10L concentrators for home and clinical use, plus portable battery-backed models for on-the-go patients. Brands: Drive, Oxyflow, Oxymed, Longfian, Homemedex, Philips, Inogen Rove 6, iGo2.

03
Rental Services

Flexible short-term and long-term rental programmes for CPAP, BPAP, and Oxygen Concentrators — making critical respiratory care accessible and affordable without large upfront investment.

04
Surgical Equipment & Accessories

A comprehensive range of basic and advanced surgical equipment alongside essential accessories — Filters, Cannula, Humidifier Bottles — ensuring complete respiratory care support for patients and clinicians.
